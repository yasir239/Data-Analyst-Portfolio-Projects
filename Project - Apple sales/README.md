# Apple Sales Data

## Project Summary
Analysis of Apple product sales to surface trends across product lines (iPhone, Mac, iPad, Accessories), compare regional performance, and measure year-over-year growth.

## Key Analyses
- Product-level sales trends and market share
- Regional/territory performance comparisons
- Year-over-year (YoY) and quarter-over-quarter (QoQ) growth rates
- Contribution of product lines to total revenue
- Seasonality and promotional impact analysis

## Tools & Technologies
- Data wrangling: Python (pandas) or Excel
- Analysis & visualization: matplotlib, seaborn, or Tableau/Power BI
- SQL for aggregations from relational stores (if available)

## Data
Files: `data/products.csv`, `data/sales.csv`, `data/stores.csv`, `data/category.csv`

## How to Use
1. Load `sales.csv` and join with product and store metadata.
2. Compute aggregates by product and region; visualize trends and growth rates.
3. Build interactive dashboards for executives to filter by region, product, and time.

## Notes
- Insights can inform inventory planning, regional promotions, and product lifecycle management.
